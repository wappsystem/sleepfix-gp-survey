Screening Questions

We invite you to complete the below questions to determine if you are eligible for the study. These questions should take approximately 2 minutes to complete. Once you complete all the questions, click the ‘Next’ button at the bottom of the page.

1. Are you aged over 18 years?

2. Are you fluent in English?

3. Do you have a smartphone?

4. Are you planning to have a baby or currently pregnant?

5. Are you a shift worker (i.e. work commencing before 6am or later than 8pm)?

6. Have you been diagnosed with epilepsy?

7. Have you been diagnosed with bipolar disorder?

8. Do you have acute or chronic pain that affects your sleep?

*If unsure, please contact the research team.
