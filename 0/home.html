Welcome to SleepFix

You have been invited to take part in an online survey exploring your perceptions and experiences with digital health interventions and sleep management.

The study is for the following registered primary health care professionals: General practitioners, pharmacists and nurses working in Australia.

The survey will collect information on your demographics, years of practice, practice size and experiences with digital health interventions and sleep management. It will take a maximum of 10 minutes to complete. As reimbursement, you will have the option to take part in a draw to win a $200 gift card (1 gift card available per health care professional group).

Your participation in this study is voluntary.
